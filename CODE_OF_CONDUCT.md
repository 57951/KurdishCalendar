# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors.

## Our Standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to creating a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:
- Discussing political, religious, or racially controversial issues
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported by contacting the project team. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
